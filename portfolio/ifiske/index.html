iFiske

iOS app for simplifying purchases of fishing permits for Swedish fishing.

What I did

My absolute first contact with Objective-C and iOS development. As part of one of my university classes, me and a handful other students were assigned to develop the first version of this fishing app that is still going strong to this day. To me it's pretty cool that my very first encounter with iOS ended up being a successful, actual app on the App Store. Other than building the initial version of the map view where we plotted out different points of interests fetched from an API I also created a dynamically populated virtual fishing permit which I was particularly proud of at the time.
