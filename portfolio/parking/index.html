Parking apps

A suite of apps focused on parking in the cities of Linköping and Skellefteå in Sweden. We first built LinPark, the parking app to be used for all parking needs within Linköping City, that acted as the prototype for the other apps. By working closely together with the product owner, Dukaten Parkering, and Swarco - the company behind an automated boom service used when entering and exiting the car parks - we developed a solid product that could easily be expanded to be used in other cities as well.

What I did

Sole iOS developer. Over the course of my 3 years at Knowit I added localization and multiple app targets for branded versions of the app, and more.
